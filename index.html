How to Play

Freeplay

click and drag the pin(it looks like an arrow) in the bottom left corner and drag over to one of the slots on the wheel. Release the mouse button to place the pin

Fill as many slots as you want then hit the START button to hear the machine play.

To start again simply press RESET

Test your skill

If you think you know how it all works. Why not test your skill?

Hit the PLAY button to hear a rhythmn and try to recreate it
